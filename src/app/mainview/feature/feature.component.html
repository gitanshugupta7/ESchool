NOTES

We know that internet connectivity is a luxury for many. Thus e-class notes are provided in th form of a simplified written version of online videolectures to make the process much easier and inclusive for all the learners. These notes are important for quick revision, accurate understanding as well as written record of the topics taught in e-school.

WEEKLY PRACTICE QUESTION

The student are provided weekly practice question to help them understand and assess each topic learned through the E-shool service. These students understand the importance of real-world application of education
